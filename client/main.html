The Forecast Factory provides a marketplace for information trading. The currency is virtual. There is no exchange of money between participants and the Forecast Factory. Participants borrow virtual money, which they can use to buy, and subsequently sell, individual $1-payoff contracts. These contracts pay $1 upon realization of a given outcome. Trading is real-time. Through skillful trading, participants can generate a positive virtual P&L (profit & loss) and get their names on the leaderboard.
