The Forecast Factory provides a marketplace for information trading. The currency is virtual. There is no exchange of actual money, neither among participants, nor between the participants and the Forecast Factory. Participants borrow virtual money up to some maximum amount, which they can use to buy, and subsequently sell, individual $1-payoff contracts. These contracts pay $1 upon realization of a given outcome. Trading is real-time. Through skillful trading, participants can generate a virtual profit (positive P&L) and get their names on the leaderboard.
